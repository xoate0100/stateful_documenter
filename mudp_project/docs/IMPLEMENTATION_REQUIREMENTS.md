# MUDP Implementation Requirements

**Purpose:** Comprehensive checklist of all contracts, documents, and tools needed to "perfect" the MUDP system

## Overview

This document provides a complete checklist of all implementation requirements to fully establish the MUDP system. These requirements are organized by the Four-Pillar Defense Architecture and Implementation Phases.

**Critical Note:** All legal documents should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before execution. This is a checklist, not legal advice.

## Pillar 1: Economic & Contractual Protection

### 1.1 Child Modeling & Likeness Licensing Agreement

**Status:** REQUIRED - Core Foundation  
**Priority:** HIGHEST

**Purpose:**
- Establishes quantifiable economic value ($500/year × 30 years = $15,000)
- Creates contractual basis for damages
- Enables tortious interference claims
- Provides legal standing

**Required Components:**
- [ ] Full contract document
- [ ] $500/year × 30-year valuation clause
- [ ] Exclusivity clause (exclusive licensing rights)
- [ ] Moral-turpitude / deepfake termination clause
- [ ] Continued asset valuation clause
- [ ] Restrictive licensing terms
- [ ] Clawback clauses
- [ ] Jurisdiction-appropriate language (Kansas/Missouri)

**Key Clauses:**
- **Valuation:** $500/year × 30 years = $15,000 total value
- **Term:** 30-year contract term
- **Termination:** Automatic termination on deepfake/misuse
- **Exclusivity:** Exclusive licensing rights to likeness
- **Renewal:** Annual renewal with payment

**Legal Review:** REQUIRED before execution

**Location:** Binder Tab 2, Section 2.1
