2. **Supervised Interactions:** All interactions between students must be supervised
3. **Class Schedule Modifications:** Schedule modifications to minimize contact
4. **Lunch/Recess Modifications:** Separate lunch and recess arrangements

1. **Academic Support:** Additional academic support as needed
2. **Flexible Attendance:** Flexible attendance policies if needed
3. **Alternative Assignments:** Alternative assignments if needed
4. **Counseling Services:** Access to school counseling services

#### Monitoring and Communication

1. **Regular Check-Ins:** Regular check-ins with school counselor or administrator
2. **Parent Communication:** Regular communication with parents about safety concerns
3. **Incident Reporting:** Clear process for reporting any incidents or concerns
4. **Documentation:** Documentation of all safety measures implemented

#### Long-Term Safety Measures

1. **Ongoing Monitoring:** Ongoing monitoring of the situation
2. **Plan Review:** Regular review and adjustment of safety plan
3. **Support Services:** Continued access to support services
4. **Protection from Retaliation:** Protection from any retaliation

- **Developed:** Within 48 hours of this request
- **Implemented:** Immediately upon development
