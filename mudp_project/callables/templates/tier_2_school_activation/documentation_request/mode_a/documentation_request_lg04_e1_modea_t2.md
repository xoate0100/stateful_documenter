## Documentation Request

### Requested Documents

We request the following documentation:

#### Title IX Policy and Procedures

1. **Title IX Policy:** Current district Title IX policy
2. **Investigation Procedures:** Detailed investigation procedures and guidelines
3. **Supportive Measures Policy:** Policy regarding supportive measures
4. **Appeal Process:** Information about appeal process (if applicable)
5. **Retaliation Policy:** Policy regarding protection from retaliation

#### Investigation Documentation

1. **Investigation Plan:** Investigation plan and timeline
2. **Investigator Information:** Name and contact information for investigator
3. **Investigation Updates:** Regular updates on investigation progress
4. **Investigation Report:** Final investigation report (when completed)
5. **Evidence Collected:** Documentation of evidence collected during investigation

#### Supportive Measures Documentation

1. **Supportive Measures Implemented:** Documentation of supportive measures implemented
2. **Safety Plan:** Written safety plan (if applicable)
3. **Academic Accommodations:** Documentation of academic accommodations (if applicable)
4. **Support Services:** Documentation of support services provided

#### School Records

1. **Student Records:** Relevant student records (subject to FERPA)
2. **Incident Reports:** Any incident reports related to this matter
3. **Disciplinary Records:** Any disciplinary records related to this matter (if applicable)
4. **Communication Records:** Relevant communication records (subject to privacy laws)

#### District Information

1. **District Legal Contact:** Contact information for district legal counsel
2. **Title IX Coordinator Contact:** Contact information for Title IX coordinator
3. **District Policy Documents:** Relevant district policy documents
4. **Insurance Information:** Information about district liability coverage (if applicable)

### Legal Basis

**Title IX Rights:**
Under Title IX and applicable regulations, we have the right to:
- Receive information about the investigation process
- Receive updates on investigation progress
- Receive information about supportive measures
- Receive the final investigation report
- Access relevant records (subject to FERPA and privacy laws)

**FERPA Rights:**
