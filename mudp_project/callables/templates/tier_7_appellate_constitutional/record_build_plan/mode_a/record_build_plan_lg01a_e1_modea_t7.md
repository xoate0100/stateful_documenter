## Record Build Plan

### Record Components

**Components to Include in Record:**

#### 1. Pleadings

- **Complaint:** Original complaint and all amendments
- **Answer:** Answer and all amendments
- **Counterclaims:** Any counterclaims and responses
- **Cross-Claims:** Any cross-claims and responses
- **Third-Party Claims:** Any third-party claims and responses

**Preservation:**
- File all pleadings with court
- Maintain copies of all pleadings
- Ensure all pleadings are in record

#### 2. Motions and Responses

- **All Motions:** All motions filed by all parties
- **All Responses:** All responses to motions
- **All Replies:** All replies to responses
- **All Orders:** All orders on motions

**Preservation:**
- File all motions and responses with court
- Maintain copies of all motions and responses
- Ensure all motions and responses are in record
- Ensure all orders are in record

#### 3. Discovery

- **Document Requests:** All document requests and responses
- **Interrogatories:** All interrogatories and responses
- **Depositions:** All deposition transcripts
- **Expert Reports:** All expert reports
- **Discovery Orders:** All discovery orders

**Preservation:**
- File all discovery documents with court (if required)
- Maintain copies of all discovery documents
- Ensure all discovery documents are in record (if required)
- Preserve all deposition transcripts

#### 4. Evidence

- **Exhibits:** All exhibits admitted or offered
- **Witness Testimony:** All witness testimony transcripts
- **Expert Testimony:** All expert testimony transcripts
- **Physical Evidence:** All physical evidence (if applicable)

**Preservation:**
- File all exhibits with court
- Maintain copies of all exhibits
- Ensure all exhibits are in record
- Preserve all testimony transcripts

#### 5. Trial Proceedings

- **Trial Transcripts:** Complete trial transcripts
- **Jury Instructions:** All jury instructions (if applicable)
- **Verdict Forms:** All verdict forms (if applicable)
- **Judgment:** Final judgment and all post-judgment orders

**Preservation:**
- Order complete trial transcripts
- Maintain copies of all trial documents
- Ensure all trial documents are in record
- Preserve all post-judgment orders

#### 6. Constitutional Issues

- **Constitutional Motions:** All motions raising constitutional issues
- **Constitutional Responses:** All responses to constitutional motions
- **Constitutional Rulings:** All rulings on constitutional issues
- **Constitutional Evidence:** All evidence relevant to constitutional issues

**Preservation:**
- File all constitutional documents with court
- Maintain copies of all constitutional documents
- Ensure all constitutional documents are in record
- Preserve all constitutional evidence

### Preservation Strategy

**General Preservation Principles:**

1. **File Everything:**
   - File all documents with court
   - Ensure all documents are in record
   - Maintain complete record

2. **Transcribe Everything:**
   - Order transcripts of all proceedings
   - Ensure all proceedings are transcribed
   - Preserve all transcripts

3. **Document Everything:**
   - Document all arguments and objections
   - Preserve all rulings on record
   - Maintain complete documentation

4. **Preserve Early:**
   - Begin record building early in case
   - Preserve issues as they arise
   - Do not wait until appeal

**Specific Preservation Tactics:**

1. **Motion Practice:**
   - File all motions with court
   - Ensure all motions are in record
   - Preserve all rulings

2. **Trial Objections:**
   - Make all objections on record
   - Ensure all objections are transcribed
   - Preserve all rulings

3. **Post-Trial Motions:**
   - File all post-trial motions
   - Ensure all post-trial motions are in record
   - Preserve all rulings

4. **Constitutional Preservation:**
   - Raise all constitutional issues on record
   - Ensure all constitutional arguments are preserved
   - Preserve all constitutional rulings
