## Discovery Plan

### Discovery Objectives

**Primary Objectives:**
1. **Establish Defendant's Conduct:**
   - Obtain evidence of defendant's creation, distribution, or use of content
   - Establish defendant's knowledge and intent
   - Document defendant's actions and communications

2. **Establish Causation and Damages:**
   - Document harm caused to plaintiff
   - Establish causal connection between defendant's conduct and harm
   - Quantify economic and non-economic damages

3. **Preserve Evidence:**
   - Ensure all relevant evidence is preserved
   - Prevent spoliation of evidence
   - Document evidence chain of custody

4. **Support Legal Claims:**
   - Gather evidence supporting each cause of action
   - Establish elements of each claim
   - Support damages claims

**Methods to be Used:**
1. **Document Requests:** Comprehensive document requests
2. **Interrogatories:** Written interrogatories
3. **Depositions:** Depositions of key witnesses
4. **Expert Discovery:** Expert witness discovery
5. **Inspection:** Inspection of property and devices
