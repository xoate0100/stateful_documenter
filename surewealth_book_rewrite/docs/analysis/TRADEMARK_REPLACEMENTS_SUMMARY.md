## Usage Guidelines

### When to Use Each Replacement

**Building Your Bank**:
- When discussing recapturing interest
- When addressing financial independence
- When explaining borrowing from yourself
- Best for: Executives, Business Owners, Dual-Income Professionals

**SureWealth Store**:
- When explaining policy mechanics
- When discussing dividend crediting
- When showing how money flows in/out
- Best for: All personas

**Accessible Funds**:
- When reframing policy loans
- When removing shame/obligation
- When explaining fund access
- Best for: All personas (especially Widow/Caregiver)

**SureWealth Stash**:
- When addressing "rate of return" objections
- When explaining "save to spend" vs "save to save"
- When emphasizing intentional access
- Best for: All personas (folksy, relatable)
