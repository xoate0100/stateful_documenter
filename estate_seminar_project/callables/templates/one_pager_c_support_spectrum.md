## Purpose

This one-pager presents three paths for estate planning support. You choose the path that fits your situation. All paths are valid—there's no "right" or "wrong" choice.

---

## Three Paths

### Path 1: Self-Directed

**What it is:**
- Use the worksheets yourself
- Review and complete independently
- Make decisions on your own
- Update as needed

**Who it's for:**
- People who prefer DIY approach
- Simple situations
- Those who want full control
- People comfortable with self-management

**What you need:**
- Worksheets from today's seminar
- Time to complete them
- Willingness to review and update

**Support level:** None required—fully self-directed

---

### Path 2: Guided Validation

**What it is:**
- Complete worksheets yourself
- Get a second set of eyes to review
- Professional validation of your work
- Peace of mind without full coordination

**Who it's for:**
- People who want to do it themselves but want validation
- Those who want professional review
- People who want to ensure nothing is missed
- Those who want confidence in their decisions

**What you need:**
- Completed worksheets
- Willingness to share your work
- Openness to feedback

**Support level:** Review and validation only

---

### Path 3: Comprehensive Coordination

**What it is:**
- Full coordination of complex situations
- Integration of retirement, tax, and legacy planning
- Professional guidance throughout
- Ongoing support and updates

**Who it's for:**
- Complex asset situations
- Business owners
- Multiple properties or accounts
- Those who want full professional coordination
- People coordinating retirement and estate planning

**What you need:**
- Completed worksheets (starting point)
- Willingness to work with professionals
- Complex situation requiring coordination

**Support level:** Full professional coordination

## How to Choose

**Consider:**
- Complexity of your situation
- Your comfort level with DIY
- Your time and capacity
- Your desire for professional support
- Your budget and preferences

**Remember:**
- All paths are valid
- You can change paths later
- No urgency to decide
- Support is optional
- You're in control
