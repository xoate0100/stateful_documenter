## Compliance Framework Summary

### "No Sales" Rules

**✅ Allowed:**
- Education, frameworks, neutral explanations
- Tools/worksheets for independent use
- Permission-based support statements
- "If you ever get stuck..." language

**❌ Not Allowed:**
- Product/package pitches, pricing, scarcity
- Testimonials as conversion proof
- Case studies ending in "then they hired us"
- Booking links on main slides

### Guardrails

1. **No Legal Advice:** Always include disclaimer, encourage attorney consultation
2. **Do Not Overclaim:** Only state what's supported about Legacy Lock/SureWealth
3. **Trust & Skepticism:** Acknowledge, reinforce autonomy, provide real tools

### Canonical CTA

**"Your next step is to complete one worksheet this week. If you get stuck and want help, we're available—no obligation."**

- Only CTA allowed
- Permission-based
- No urgency
- Preserves educational contract
